About Pty PlumbingCo

Our Story

Established in 2015, Pty PlumbingCo has been serving the Polokwane community with excellence and reliability. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted plumbing service providers in the region.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional plumbing services with integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experience: Over 30 years of combined expertise
  • Professional Team: Licensed and certified plumbers
  • Quality Work: We stand behind all our work
  • Affordable Pricing: Transparent quotes, no hidden charges
  • 24/7 Emergency Service: Available when you need us
  • Local Knowledge: Familiar with Polokwane's infrastructure

Our Values

We believe in honest communication, quality workmanship, and treating every customer with respect. Your satisfaction is our priority.
